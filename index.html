about

Hello and a warm welcome! My name is Ed. I'm a software engineer, and this is a little bit about me.

Sparing any backstory, it began in ancient times when I graduated the University of Maryland with a BS in Computer Engineering..

My interests back then included network protocols, OS programming, and cryptography.

Things then took a drastic turn, as I came upon a tremendous opportunity in the completely unrelated field of real estate. I spent a number of years administering, buying, and selling various properties and investments. This gave me a great deal of experience with teamwork, managing people, and maximizing their skills. I also took advantage of the flexible schedule to do some traveling and learn a foreign language..

Eventually, after a lot of effort, I made these properties and investments self-sustaining. This left me with plenty of free time and a yearning for something to do with it. There it was that my original passion for programming was re-ignited. I remembered that I'd always enjoyed web programming and was in need of a refresher in general, so I went ahead and did one of those infamous bootcamps..

And thus, we arrive at the present day.
